Who are we?

Indie Aid is a program to assist small developers with Publishing, Optimization, Funding, Connections, and Development . We plan to outgrow the stigma surrounding the gaming industry, and the big-time developers making a bad name for it by helping small developers with original concepts to bring a new age to the industry. If you are curious about which games we have supported check them out here. We detail our full message on our About Us webpage.






Why should you care?

The gaming industry has been a large part of many peoples lives - both consumer and developers. In recent years, the treatment of both the consumer and developer by large corporations has spiraled out of control. The mistreatment of these developers, and the consumer being fed repetitive, underdeveloped video games has led to an upsurge of Indie Developers taking the means into their own hands. These indie developers could lead the gaming industry to somewhere great, because now more than ever creative people have the power to make amazing video games!
