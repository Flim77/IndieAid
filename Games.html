At Indie Aid, we plan to expand our group of supported games using your donations. Using these donations, we can further fund small developers to continue making the games we love. This is a list concurrent with what games and developers we are actively supporting right now.


Drama Storage is a recently released small indie game by RushJustice . This dev team is a small 3 person co-op out of Arizona, and have seen large success in making storytelling cleaning games.


Scrap Confusion is the final installment of the Scrap trilogy by WePossible finishes the long-running franchise, releasing March 18th 2024. WePossible is a two man team from Indonesia, where they plan to make Game Development a full time job with the release of Scrap Confusion.


Constant Encouragement by BeePriority is a retro-styled party game, playing off of the nostalgic styles of N64 party games with a modern twist. BeePriority is a four man team put together out of Siberia, Russia. The group of developers were once ice fishers, but after fishing all of the fish they turned to game development. Using Indie Aid, they were able to kickstart their studio.


Salary Cut by TOPraid is a classic Boomer shoot em' up, spinning off popular movement shooters, where the player has to destroy evil autonomous office equipment. TOPraid is a one man team with a big heart for stylization, and consumer feedback. They have been one of the original supporters, and supported since Indie Aid's inception.


Cold Undertaker by Articulate Approach is a turn based RPG for Mobile and PC devices. Articulate Approach is a two man team out of South Africa, in Grade 7, developing high quality top down beat-em-up's.


Dump Deficiency by Charter Stab is a realistic driving simulator set in the year 2102, where the world is in a dump truck driver shortage. Charter Stab is a 8 man studio based out of California, after they all put their college funds into making a game studio to follow their passion.


Talk Date by DevSlant is a psychological thriller with the guise of a dating simulator, where it speaks on topics such as mental health and self appreciation. DevSlant is a one man team from Canada, making small indie games in their meantime. We are actively helping them make a side income from their video games to support their family.


Noise Battery by Shallow Musical is a Roguelite puzzle game, making strides in the indie game sphere with their interesting game mechanics and original style. Shadow Musical is another one man Indie Developmer, with their hit game Noise Battery, they plan to switch their income entirely to game design.
